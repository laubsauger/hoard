**Core concept**

A cinematic isometric zombie-survival game where an authored city sits above a hidden destructible structural grid. The player can breach, reinforce, burn, dismantle, obstruct, and repurpose existing spaces. Every modification changes navigation, sight, sound, structural safety, shelter viability, and horde behavior.

The game uses graphic survival realism: modern materials and lighting, painterly value control, selective strong outlines, readable silhouettes, explicit gore, individual anatomical damage, and massive tiered hordes. The underlying architecture uses low-cost abstractions wherever they remain invisible and behaviorally consistent.
